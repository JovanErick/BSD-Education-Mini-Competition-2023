Kanawa Island

Last Updated: 21 May 2023

Kanawa Island is a small island off the coast of Flores. The underwater beauty of this island is well-known. This island is surrounded by several types of coral reefs, as well as white sand and pure sea water, which attracts tourists.


To get to Kanawa Island, you must first land at Komodo Aiport, Labuan Bajo. After that, you can travel to the dock by speedboat for about an hour. You can also charter a boat that will arrive in approximately an hour.



This island offers a wide range of activities, such as snorkeling, diving, and fishing. Kanawa Island's management has also prepared snorkeling equipment, so you won't have to worry if you wish to explore the underwater world.


When snorkeling or diving, you can see and interact with fish and other water animals like crabs, starfish, and small fish. There are also many other coral reefs that are really lovely along the ocean.


Not only is the underwater beauty spectacular, but there is also a hill that showcases Kanawa Island's splendor. You can ascend the hill in the middle of the island. The path is not tough because there are no tall trees on the hill, only grass. You may capture the occasion with an exquisite island backdrop there.


Another thing you may do is watch sunrise and sunset. To see the sunrise, you must arrive on the island at 5:00 WITA. Kanawa will provide the allure of a stunning sunrise that can warm your body. Kanawa captures the sunset even in the afternoon. Kanawa's "golden sunset" charm can bring calm to anyone who views it.
