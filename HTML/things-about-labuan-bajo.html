Things You Need to Know About Labuan Bajo

Last Updated: 20 May 2023

Labuan Bajo is an undiscovered gem in eastern Indonesia. The village is located in Komodo County, West Manggarai County, East Nusa Tenggara Province, and is separated from West Nusa South by the Sape Strait. Labuan Bajo is one of five super-priority destinations in Indonesia that are being developed.


This place is the entry to Komodo National Park, which maintains the world's great natural beauty and ancient wildlife. From the indigenous Comodo species on Rinca and Komodo Islands to a line of exotic islands, underwater wildlife, and Aduhai beaches, a vacation in Labuan Bajo allows you to see it all.


Another way to appreciate Labuan Bajo is to gaze at the sky. This ideal sunset viewing location is not far from Komodo International Airport. To see the beautiful sky that shocks the soul, charming friends can choose the destinations of Love Hill, Amelia's Peak, and Silvia's Peak. There's also the Rangko Cave, which has the allure of an oasis with its refreshing water pools.



Entrance to Komodo National Park

Labuan Bajo and Komodo National Park are inseparable. Because they are linked, it is recommended to visit Komodo National Park while the attractive companion is in Labuan Bajo. Charming partners can travel there via ferry or fast boat. There are numerous departure times available, ranging from early morning to late at night.


Komodo National Park, designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1991, includes Komodo Island, Rinca Island, Padar Island, and several other nearby islands. In 1912, a scientific magazine reported on the presence of the komodo (Varanus komodoensis), a large frog, in the world for the first time. Pieter Antonie Ouwens, director of the Bogor Zoological Museum, wrote the journal. The discovery marked the beginning of Labuan Bajo's life in the eyes of the world, as many visitors and scientists came to witness the Ora, the inhabitants' comodo.


Aside from Komodo, this national park is also known for its amazing panoramas. If you are a hobby photographer, then it is necessary to include a few spots here as places to photograph.
